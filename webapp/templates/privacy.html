Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Dray Trucking ("we", "our", or "us") provides a mobile application designed for managing drayage and logistics operations. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the app.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Username, company (SCAC), and login credentials
  • Operational Data: Container information, dispatch records, driver data, and related logistics information
  • Contact Information: Driver names and contact details as entered by authorized users
  • Device Information: Basic device and usage data for app functionality and troubleshooting

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the app’s core functionality
  • Authenticate users and manage secure access
  • Support logistics and dispatch operations
  • Improve performance and reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared only:

  • Within your organization as part of normal app usage
  • With service providers necessary to operate the app
  • When required by law or legal process

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including secure authentication and data handling practices. However, no system is completely secure.

Data Retention

Information is retained as long as necessary to provide services and meet operational or legal requirements.

User Responsibility

Access to the app is restricted to authorized users. Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials.

Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for business use and is not directed toward children under 13.
