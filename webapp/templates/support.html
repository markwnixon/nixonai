Dray Trucking Support

Welcome to the support page for the Dray Trucking mobile app. This app is designed to help manage drayage operations, including container tracking, dispatch workflows, driver information, operational documents, and customer account activity.

Support Email: mark@draytrucking.com

Support Hours: Monday - Friday, 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM Eastern Time

Common Support Topics

  • Login assistance
  • Password reset help
  • SCAC/company access issues
  • Questions about dispatch, containers, drivers, or balances
  • Reporting app errors or unexpected behavior

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this app for?
Dray Trucking is intended for authorized users managing drayage and logistics operations.

Why can’t I log in?
Access requires a valid username, password, and the correct company SCAC code. If you need help, contact support.

How do I report a problem?
Email mark@draytrucking.com with a description of the issue, your device type, and screenshots if available.

How do I request account access?
Account access is managed by your company administrator or system administrator.
