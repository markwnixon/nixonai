The Class8 Framework

Class8 is the centerpiece of the platform. It provides the operational database, management screens, reporting tools, and API foundation that support the Dray Trucking mobile app and the AI-agentic automation system.

Operations Management

Manage trucking jobs, containers, dispatch workflows, customer information, schedules, manifests, documents, and port-related activity from one web-based system.

Financial & Compliance Control

Keep financial records, open balances, reports, compliance paperwork, assets, permits, filings, and required documentation connected to real operations.

Automation Ready

Class8 supports automated agents that monitor email, update jobs, process work orders, help generate quotes, download gate tickets, and support invoice packaging.

How Work Moves Through the System

Class8 is designed around the real flow of drayage operations, from customer request to dispatch, port activity, billing, accounting, and reporting.

1

Work Arrives

Orders, quote requests, delivery orders, and operational updates enter the system.

2

Jobs Are Managed

Dispatchers manage containers, trips, drivers, schedules, paperwork, and status.

3

Automation Acts

Agents can monitor email, process documents, trigger workflows, and update records.

4

Billing Is Packaged

Gate tickets, documents, invoices, and supporting records are organized together.

5

Reports Stay Current

Accounting, balances, compliance, and operating reports reflect current business activity.
